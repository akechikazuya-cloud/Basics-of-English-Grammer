Masculine and Feminine Nouns

Masculine nouns are words for men, boys, and male animals.
Feminine nouns are words for women, girls, and female animals.

man                woman

actor              actress

Common Gender Nouns

Many nouns can be used for both males and females. These are called common gender nouns.

teacher
baby
doctor
scientist

parent
astronaut
president
child

dancer

Masculine and Feminine Nouns for Animals

With animals, there is usually one general name for the animal, plus separate words for the male and the female. Sometimes the male name matches the general name. Sometimes the female name matches the general name.

Animal             Masculine          Feminine
rabbit             buck               doe
horse              stallion           mare
sheep              ram                ewe
pig                boar               sow
chicken            rooster            hen
duck               drake              duck
cattle             bull               cow
goose              gander             goose
fox                fox                vixen
tiger              tiger              tigress
lion               lion               lioness
